# Carnival of 3

A horror card game made in PICO-8. Black and white only (1 bit).

---

You wander into a carnival that shouldn't be there. A fat black cat sits behind a table with three cards and invites you to play. You don't remember agreeing to this.

The game is simple — pick the right card, win gold, survive the night. But the cat has a way of making things complicated. Every time you win it offers you something. Some of those things are upgrades. Some are not. You'll be able to tell the difference. Doesn't mean you'll have a choice.

Tied to the table are three balloons. Those are your soul. Lose a round, one pops. Lose all three and the cat stops pretending to be friendly.

To actually escape you need 100 gold and four curses on your back. Yes, curses. That part will make sense later. Probably.

Whatever is inside that carnival carriage behind the cat — and something is definitely in there — you do not want to find out what happens if you run out of balloons.

---


**Made with PICO-8.**
